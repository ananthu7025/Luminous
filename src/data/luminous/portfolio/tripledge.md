TripLedge came to Luminous Logics with a coordination problem. Managing snow removal operations across North Battleford, Saskatchewan meant juggling employee scheduling, route assignments, grading records, equipment tracking, and client billing — across multiple tools that didn't talk to each other.

Supervisors were making decisions based on outdated information. Field crews had no mobile visibility into their assignments. Billing was happening days after job completion.

We built TripLedge — a unified web and mobile platform that connects the office to the field in real time, from route dispatch to invoice.
