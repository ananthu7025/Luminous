SpiceMagic had exceptional products but a digital presence that was actively working against them. Their existing WooCommerce store looked generic, loaded slowly on mobile, and gave customers no reason to choose them over Amazon.

We rebuilt their entire digital storefront as a custom Next.js experience on Shopify's backend — designed around what we call sensory e-commerce: making the customer feel the product before they taste it.
